Who reads this

How questions are handled

Questions submitted here are reviewed by program coordinators and, where relevant, passed to participating Elders and Aboriginal educators for a considered response. Responses are shared in group sessions rather than individually — this keeps the question box a community space rather than a one-on-one service. Urgent concerns about safety or wellbeing are referred to the school's support team.

This question box is not a crisis, counselling or legal support service. It is for program feedback, cultural questions, access issues and learning support. Any safety, wellbeing or mandatory-reporting concern will be referred to Mian School’s existing wellbeing procedures.

Useful prompts

Questions can be practical, cultural, educational or access-focused. For example:

  • What would make the sessions feel safe and respectful?
  • Which local stories or cultural practices do you want to learn more about?
  • How can we make it easier to access the program from home or in the community?
  • What does a meaningful measure of success look like to you?

Submissions are reviewed by program coordinators. Responses are shared in sessions. For urgent concerns, please speak directly with a teacher or school counsellor.
