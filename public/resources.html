Resource Library

Trusted services and supports

A curated list of organisations relevant to students, educators, families and community partners involved in or connected to the Cultural Connection Program.

Partner Organisations

Justice and reintegration support

Legal & Financial

Miranda Project — Community Restorative Centre

Provides legal, financial and reintegration support to women facing or recovering from incarceration. A key partner in the broader support ecosystem this program connects to.

Rehabilitation

Corrective Services NSW

Offers post-release rehabilitation and reintegration services grounded in principles of restorative practice. Also delivers risk-based offender assessment and community supervision.

Arts & Storytelling

Red Room Poetry — Luke Patterson

Uses storytelling rooted in First Nations knowledge as a diversionary and prevention tool. A direct creative complement to the cultural immersion approach of this program.

Education and Cultural Programs

Learning and cultural support

Scholarships

Australian Indigenous Education Foundation

Provides scholarships for First Nations students pursuing secondary and tertiary education. Funded in part by the Australian Government's Closing the Gap commitment of $20 million.

Digital Access

Rural Access Gap Program — Digital NSW

Works to improve digital inclusion in rural and regional communities. Has improved student device ratios in rural areas from 1:6 to 1:4. Directly relevant to ensuring equitable access to this program's digital platform.

Policy

National Agreement on Closing the Gap (2020)

The national policy framework within which this program operates. Outcome 5 (Year 12 completion) and Outcome 6 (tertiary qualification) are the two most directly relevant targets.

Cultural

Local Wiradjuri Community Organisations — Dubbo region

Elders, community groups and cultural organisations connected to Wiradjuri Country. Program facilitation is designed around local relationships, not centralised external delivery. Contact via the Question Box to connect.

Access Guidance

Using the digital platform in Dubbo

At School

The program website is accessible on school computers during class and study periods. Teachers can request dedicated digital access sessions.

Dubbo City Library

Free computer access is available at Dubbo City Library. The question box and resource library are fully functional without a personal device.

Mobile-Friendly

The site is designed to work on mobile phones. Students with limited home internet can access offline content or use community Wi-Fi at libraries and community centres.
